About the Legend
Who is CR7?

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. Growing up in poverty, he channelled every hardship into fuel for greatness on the football pitch.

From a skinny teenager at Sporting CP to the most decorated footballer of all time, Ronaldo's journey is a masterclass in discipline, sacrifice, and relentless self-improvement. He didn't just play football — he reinvented what a footballer could be.

His famous work ethic — staying after training, perfecting free kicks, obsessing over his physical condition — turned natural talent into something superhuman. There is no player in history who has combined goals, consistency, and longevity the way CR7 has.

The Journey
Career Timeline
2002
Sporting CP — The Beginning
Made his professional debut at 17 for Sporting CP in Portugal. His dazzling display against Manchester United in a friendly changed his life forever.
2003
Manchester United — Sir Alex Calls
Signed by Sir Alex Ferguson for £12.24 million. Wore the iconic #7 shirt. Won 3 Premier League titles, 1 Champions League, and his first Ballon d'Or.
2009
Real Madrid — The Galáctico Era
Joined Real Madrid for a then world-record £80 million. Scored 450 goals in 438 games. Won 4 Champions League titles and 4 Ballon d'Or awards.
2016
Euro 2016 — National Hero
Led Portugal to their first ever European Championship title, cementing his status as the greatest Portuguese footballer of all time.
2018
Juventus — Serie A Dominance
Moved to Juventus for €100 million. Won 2 Serie A titles and scored his famous bicycle kick against Juventus — even Juve fans gave him a standing ovation.
2021
Return to Manchester United
Returned to Old Trafford in an emotional homecoming, becoming the all-time top scorer in men's international football.
2023
Al Nassr — A New Chapter
Joined Saudi club Al Nassr, continuing to break scoring records well into his late 30s, proving age is just a number when you're CR7.
